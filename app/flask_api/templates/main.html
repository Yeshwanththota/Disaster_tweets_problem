Welcome to our Tweet Classifier!

Twitter has become an important communication channel in times of emergency. The ubiquitousness of smartphones enables people to announce an emergency they are observing in real-time. Because of this, more agencies are interested in programmatically monitoring Twitter (i.e. disaster relief organizations and news agencies). But, its not always clear whether a persons words are actually announcing a disaster. In this app, we use a machine learning model that predicts which Tweets are about real disasters and which ones are not. For example: "The flood in Nigeria was terrible" is a real disaster Tweet, while "I am hungry and need food" is not. Try it out(Tweet in English)!
